About Nicole Nguyen.

I love designing and developing great user interfaces.

I never forget a good story. At the center of every product is a user and their story. Who are they? Why would they use this product? What delights them? I've always been fascinated with how people interact with one another and with technology.

I studied Product & Design at Startup Institute Boston and sociology at the University of Washington. My background includes marketing, technical support, user research and a whole lot of espresso. I'm inspired by vibrant colors, great photography, and music - that's my ukulele above! I am particularly interested in social good (think education or non-profit) and women founded startups.
